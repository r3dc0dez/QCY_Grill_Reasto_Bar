Our Legacy

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Pagudpud, QCY Grill Resto & Bar has become synonymous with exceptional grilling and authentic Filipino flavors. What started as a family dream has evolved into one of Ilocos Norte's most distinctive dining destinations.
