Our Legacy

Founded in 2020 in the heart of Pagudpud, QCY Grill Resto & Bar has become a beloved destination for both locals and tourists seeking authentic Ilocano cuisine and modern Asian fusion dishes. Located in the scenic Sitio Baog 1, our restaurant offers a perfect blend of traditional flavors and contemporary dining experience.
